Our Story

About Luxe Haven

A legacy of excellence, a tradition of luxury, and a commitment to unforgettable experiences since 1925.

Our Heritage

Nearly a Century of
Exceptional Hospitality

Founded in 1925 by the visionary hotelier Sir William Beaumont, Luxe Haven began as a modest boutique hotel that quickly became the destination of choice for dignitaries, celebrities, and discerning travelers from around the world.

What started as a 20-room establishment has grown into a landmark of luxury, yet we've never forgotten our roots. Every guest who walks through our doors is still treated with the same personal attention and care that Sir William envisioned nearly a century ago.

Today, under the stewardship of the third generation of the Beaumont family, Luxe Haven continues to set the standard for luxury accommodation, blending timeless elegance with contemporary sophistication.
