Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged upon request, subject to availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast is included in our Suite categories and above. Standard room guests can add breakfast at our restaurant for $45 per person per day.

Do you offer airport shuttle service?

Yes, we offer 24/7 airport transfer services. Luxury sedans are available for $95 each way, and our Rolls-Royce service is available for $250 each way.

What is your cancellation policy?

Standard reservations can be cancelled up to 48 hours before arrival for a full refund. Special rates and packages may have different policies.

Are pets allowed at the hotel?

We're happy to welcome well-behaved pets under 25 lbs. Pet-friendly rooms are available with a one-time cleaning fee of $150. Service animals are always welcome.

Is there WiFi available?

Complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the hotel, including all guest rooms, public areas, and meeting spaces.
